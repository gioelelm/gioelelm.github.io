<a href="{{<ref "people/lucafusar.md">}}" target="_self">Luca Fusar Bassini</a> has been awarded the prestigious Boehringer Ingelheim Fonds (BIF) PhD Fellowship!

The BIF PhD Fellowship is one of the most competitive fellowships in biomedical research, with less than 10% of applicants receiving an award. The fellowship supports outstanding junior scientists worldwide who wish to pursue ambitious PhD projects in basic biomedical research in internationally leading laboratories.

Luca's research focuses on the lipidome-transcriptome axis in neurulation and developing computational methods to contribute to therapeutics for neurodevelopmental diseases.

Congratulations Luca on this outstanding achievement!
