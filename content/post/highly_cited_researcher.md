We are pleased to announce that Gioele La Manno has been recognized as a Highly Cited Researcher for 2023 by Clarivate Analytics.

This recognition is awarded to researchers who have demonstrated significant and broad influence in their field, as measured by highly cited papers ranking in the top 1% by citations for their field and publication year in the Web of Science.

The recognition reflects the impact of our work in single-cell genomics, RNA velocity, and computational methods for understanding brain development.
